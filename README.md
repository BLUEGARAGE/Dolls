# Dolls
Dolls AI human image &amp; video creator

https://dolls.bluegarage.site/

Create unlimited, AI generated images and videos — all for free, locally, right on your PC.

 Dolls is a fast, high-quality AI software for creating stunning human images and videos. It runs free, unlimited, and offline right on your PC — Supports multilingual prompts and every image you generate is 100% free for commercial use. The Dolls interface specializes in generating images of people, allowing you to

specify the face, body, gender, clothing, pose, and environment separately when creating an image. It can produce a wide variety of styles, from ultra-realistic images to American-comic and anime aesthetics. In addition, it includes features for AI-based partial image editing, upscaling, and even converting images into videos.
